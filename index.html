My Experience

"KPPS Member"

Work together in a team consisting of one KPPS chairperson and six KPPS members to prepare and carry out the holding of the Republic of Indonesia Election in 2024, organized by the KPU (General Election Commission), in accordance with the SOP, and the rules of state law.

Distribute 209 Model C-notification letters-KPU or voting notification letters to voters, to people registered in the DPT in the working area, accompanied by taking pictures as a form of documentation and report material.

Carry out the voting. As KPPS 3, I was tasked with preparing five types of ballots, consisting of the President and vice president, DPD RI, DPD RI, Provincial DPRD, and Regency / City DPRD, to be handed over to the community. Completing each location address data on each ballot, then submitted to KPPS 1 as chairman to be signed, then given to KPPS 2 to be stamped with TPS 6 stamp, then given to voters to be legally counted.

Implementation of vote counting. As KPPS 3, I was tasked with KPPS 4 recording each sheet of 209 used ballots announced by the KPPS chairman into the Model C-Results form, which was previously examined first to check the validity or invalidity of the votes received, then checking the recording results in accordance with the results announced by the KPPS chairman.
